My Hobbies

  Sports

I really like playing sports, especially because it relaxes me, makes me feel good and clears my mind. During the week I try to go three or four days to the gym. But what I like the most is going for a run when the weather is good.

I also really like winter sports. I used to love to go skiing, but now I prefer snowboarding since I tried it four years ago.

  Creative activities

In my free time, especially when I need to disconnect, I love to spend hours painting and drawing.

It is a hobby that I discovered during the pandemic and that currently it is more difficult for me to find time for it, but that I continue to enjoy just the same.

  Spend time outside

For me it is very important to spend time with my friends and my family, and it is what I spend most of my weekends on.

I love going out to eat and have a drink with my friends, and I also love doing activities in nature, like hiking.
